## LinkedIn

I built Match Edge, an open-source in-play decision-support calculator for the
Argentina–Spain final.

Enter the score, minute, contract price, bankroll, and commission. It estimates a
fair trophy probability, calculates a conservative maximum entry, sizes the
position with one-eighth Kelly capped at 1% of bankroll, and returns PASS when the
price does not offer enough margin.

The repo includes the derivation, proof boundaries, automated tests, and a live
demo. It is educational software—not a promise of profit—and the safest result is
often PASS.
